# beacon, tracker, adware, pup
-  Microsoft
    - c.msn.com: tracking and analytics on MSN portals (**botnet**)
    - r.bing.com: Click Tracker. the "r" stands for **Redirect**. can be malicious used.
    - bat.bing.com: the most "active" tracker. it follows your session on a third-party website. **Bing Ads Tracking**
    - browser.events.data.msn.com: uploading data. telemetry and analytics endpoint used primarily by Microsoft Edge and the Windows shell.    
- Google
    - googleads.g.doubleclick.net: Ad Personalization, It uses cookies to remember; Click Redirects: routed through this domain so Google can log. Can be used by malicious actors for **malvertising**** 
    - - www.googletagmanager.com: web analytics and marketing tags.
- sb.scprecardresearch.com: digital tracking and market research
- clientstream.launchdarkly.com: identifies your session as a "Context" (e.g., "User #54321 on Chrome") to decide which features to show you.

# privacy issue
- Microsoft
    - ecn.dev.virtualearth.net: receive your IP address and location request. **Bing Maps**
    - www.edge.com: collect "telemetry" (data about how you use the browser).
    - g.live.com: acts as an endpoint for configuration and sometimes telemetry.
    - turing-writingassistance.edge.microsoft.com: AI-powered writing tools.
    - img-s.msn.cn: delivering the thumbnails, icons, and photos.
    - go.trouter.skype.com: real-time communication and signaling.
    - config.edge.skype.com: Chat and Call in Edge
    - ntp.msn.com: New Tab Page images and layout
- Google    
    - play.google.com: Google Play Games on PC service running in the background.

# low-risk
- Microsoft
    - th.bing.com: **Thumbnails**. make pages load faster by serving small, optimized images
    - img-s-msn-com.akamaized.net: CDN for high-resolution, full-sized images. Lock Screen, Spotlight.
    - www.bing.com: massive portal for Microsoft's search.
    - nav-edge.smartscreen.microsoft.com: technical endpoint used by the Microsoft Edge browser for the Microsoft Defender SmartScreen service.
- Google
    - ogads-pa.client6.google.com: cookie handling.
    - signaler-pa.client6.google.com: keeps session alive and in sync.
    - www.google.com: homepage for Google Search.
    - recaptcha.net: legitimate Google service used to distinguish human users.
    - jnn-pa.googleapis.com: Job Notification Network (JNN). It is a high-efficiency background signaling channel used by Google services.
